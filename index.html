Rental requirements

Drivers must be at least 22 years old and present a valid driver’s license from their country of residence. Tourists should also present a passport, and any major credit card used for deposit and payment must be in the renter’s own name.

Accepted payment methods

J & J King Auto Rentals accepts Mastercard, Visa, American Express, and Discover.

Fuel policy

Fuel is same-to-same. Please return the vehicle with the same fuel level provided at pickup or refueling charges will apply.

Insurance coverage

Comprehensive insurance coverage is offered with every rental so guests and families can drive with confidence during their stay.

Cancellation policy

You may cancel up to 24 hours before scheduled pickup with no cancellation fee. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before pickup may incur a fee, and no-shows may be charged up to the first day’s rate.

Additional services

Additional drivers may be added to the rental agreement for an extra fee if they meet the same rental qualifications and are present at pickup. Children’s booster seats and infant car seats are also available upon request.

Helpful answers

Common questions for travelers.

This FAQ now works alongside the booking form and rental terms so guests can get more answers before contacting you.

What are the requirements for renting a vehicle?

You must be at least 22 years old, hold a valid driver’s license from your country of residence, and provide a passport if you are visiting as a tourist. Any major credit card used for the deposit and payment must be in your own name.

Which payment methods are accepted?

We accept Mastercard, Visa, American Express, and Discover.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup with no cancellation fee. If you cancel less than 24 hours before pickup, a cancellation fee may apply, and no-shows may be charged a fee that can equal the first day’s rental rate.

What happens if flights are delayed?

No problem. Please call with your updated flight information and we will do our best to accommodate your new arrival time.

What is your fuel policy?

Fuel is same-to-same. Please return the vehicle with the same amount of fuel it had at pickup or refueling charges will apply.

Do you offer insurance?

Yes. We offer comprehensive insurance coverage with every rental so you and your family are protected while driving our vehicle.

Can we add another driver?

Yes. Additional drivers may be added to the rental agreement for an extra fee. They must meet the same rental qualifications as the primary driver and be present at pickup to be added to the contract.

What happens if I return the vehicle late?

A late-return fee may apply. To avoid additional charges, please contact us in advance if you expect to be delayed.

Do you offer children’s car seats?

Yes. Children’s booster seats and infant car seats are available upon request with your reservation.
